ALPHAGROWTH PROPOSAL STATUS

A disciplined path
from readiness to growth.

PRIME front-loads technical diligence, holds most funding behind a public release gate, and measures the result on-chain. Unused, unearned, or unreleased funds return to the Cardano Treasury under six defined triggers.

01

READINESS

Audit the rails

Review protocols, infrastructure, liquidity routes, and integration readiness before capital is deployed.

02

DESIGN

Close the gaps

Turn the audit into public recommendations, partner plans, and a deployment roadmap with clear owners.

03

EXECUTION

Deploy and measure

Release gated capital only after the Month-4 decision, then tie execution economics to verified growth.
